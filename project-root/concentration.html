The Accounting Concentration

This timeline shows the recommended courses for each year to complete the accounting concentration at Babson.

Year 1

  • ACC1000 - Intro to Financial Accounting

Year 2

  • ACC2002 - Managerial Accounting
  • ACC3500 - Intermediate Accounting I

Year 3

  • ACC3501 - Intermediate Accounting II
  • ACC4530 - Advanced Accounting

Year 4

  • Electives such as Auditing, Taxes, Tax Policy, Modeling with Excel, etc.
  • (Must take at least one elective to fulfill concentration requirements.)

See the official Babson accounting website for full requirements.
