Meet the professors guiding your journey through accounting at Babson.

Shay Blachette Proulx

Professor Blanchette Proulx is a former PwC Assurance Partner, and she brings 20+ years of experience in public accounting. Her expertise includes auditing, financial reporting, and advising private companies through IPOs and strategic growth.

Go Attachot

Professor Attachot is an Assistant Professor of Practice in Accounting. Originally from Thailand, he brings global experience from ExxonMobil and holds a Ph.D. in Accounting. His research focuses on international financial reporting, and he teaches financial and managerial accounting at Babson.

Cristina Alberti

Professor Alberti is an Assistant Professor of Accounting at Babson and a former PwC Assurance Manager. Her research explores how audit firm and academic cultures impact decision-making and diversity. She teaches auditing and financial accounting.
