Accounting Careers

Public vs. Private Accounting

Public Accounting: Typically involves client-facing services such as auditing, tax prep, and consulting. Often associated with firms like the Big 4 (PwC, EY, Deloitte, KPMG).

Private Accounting: Involves internal financial management within a company. Common roles include budgeting, cost accounting, and financial analysis.

Main Pathways

  • Audit: Examining financial records for accuracy and compliance.
  • Tax: Preparing and advising on tax strategies for clients or employers.
  • Advisory: Providing strategic business insights and risk management solutions.
